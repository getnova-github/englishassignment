How can I make a new assignment?


It's quite simple!
1. Go to github, sign up for an account
2. Go here
3. Press code
4. Press codespaces
5. Create a new codespace by pressing +
6. Wait
7. Click port
8. Click the globe icon in the 2nd column of 6080
9. Enter the passcode vscode
10. The Google doc has now been created!
